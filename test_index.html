About Us

DailyBite is a nutrition tracking application that helps you monitor your daily food intake, analyze nutritional patterns, and achieve your health goals.

Our platform provides an intuitive interface for recording meals, visualizing nutrition data, and selectively sharing your progress with friends, family, or healthcare professionals.

Key Features

Food Tracking

Record your meals and automatically calculate calories and nutrients.

Data Analysis

Visualize your nutrition patterns and track progress over time.

Selective Sharing

Share your achievements and progress with friends or nutritionists.
